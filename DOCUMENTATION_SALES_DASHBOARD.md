# Sales & Product Analytics Dashboard Documentation

## Overview

The Sales & Product Analytics Dashboard offers a comprehensive view of your sales data, product performance, and customer segments. Built for advanced business intelligence, it empowers decision-makers to identify trends, optimize product offerings, and understand customer behavior across regions.

## Components

- **Treemap:** Visualizes product count by category and country for market distribution analysis
- **Donut Chart:** Shows monthly sales trades by country for trend spotting
- **KPI Card:** Tracks category and customer goals in real time
- **Product Performance Bar Chart:** Highlights top products by total sales
- **Customer Segments Bar Chart:** Analyzes customer segments and unit price distribution

## Data Structure

- **Category:** Product category (e.g., Electronics, Home, Sportswear)
- **Country:** Country of sale (e.g., USA, Canada, UK, Germany)
- **ProductName:** Name of the product
- **CustomerID:** Unique identifier for each customer
- **Segment:** Customer segment classification
- **TotalSales:** Total sales value for each product
- **UnitPrice:** Price per unit
- **Month:** Month of sale

## Usage Instructions

1. Import your sales, product, and customer data into the dashboard.
2. Use filters to focus on specific categories, countries, or customer segments.
3. Analyze the visualizations to identify high-performing products, sales trends, and customer opportunities.

## Advanced Customization

- Integrate with live data sources for real-time analytics
- Add calculated fields for deeper insights (e.g., profit margin, growth rate)
- Customize color schemes and layouts to match your brand
- Expand with additional KPIs or drill-downs as needed
